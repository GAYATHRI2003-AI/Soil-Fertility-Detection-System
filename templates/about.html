About FertilityPro

Revolutionizing Soil Analysis & Agricultural Intelligence

What is FertilityPro?

FertilityPro is an AI-powered soil fertility analysis and agricultural decision support system designed to help farmers, agronomists, and agricultural professionals make data-driven decisions for optimal crop productivity. By combining advanced machine learning, deep learning, and large language models with comprehensive agricultural knowledge, FertilityPro provides actionable insights for sustainable farming practices across India.

Our Mission

Empower farmers with accurate, real-time soil analysis and recommendations to increase productivity while maintaining environmental sustainability.

Our Vision

Transform agriculture through intelligent technology, making precision soil management accessible to every farmer regardless of their technical expertise.

Our Values

Accuracy, Sustainability, Accessibility, Innovation, and Farmer-Centric Design guide every decision we make.

Advanced Technology Stack

FertilityPro leverages cutting-edge AI and machine learning technologies

Machine Learning (ML)

RandomForestClassifier

Ensemble learning algorithm with 80% accuracy for soil fertility classification. Trained on 1,000+ soil samples across 15+ Indian states, analyzing 10 soil parameters to identify optimal, high, moderate, low, and infertile soil conditions.

Deep Learning (DL)

all-MiniLM-L6-v2 Embeddings

State-of-the-art sentence transformer (90MB) for semantic text understanding. Converts agricultural knowledge into 384-dimensional embeddings for intelligent information retrieval and similarity matching across 5,691 indexed document chunks.

Large Language Model (LLM)

Google FLAN-T5-Base

250-million parameter instruction-following model for natural language understanding. Synthesizes complex agricultural knowledge into clear, actionable answers (~50ms inference). Operates locally without cloud dependency, ensuring privacy and reliability.

Vector Database

ChromaDB

Scalable vector search engine optimized for semantic retrieval. Indexes 5,691 chunks from 35+ research papers across 13 categories (150 MB total). Enables fast, context-aware knowledge retrieval in <2 seconds.

Key Features

AI-Powered Analysis

Analyze 10 soil parameters (N, P, K, pH, EC, OC, S, Zn, Fe, B) using Liebig's Law of Minimum to identify limiting factors.

Real-Time Insights

Get instant fertility assessments, precise amendment recommendations, and soil improvement strategies tailored to your field.

LLM-Powered Q&A

Ask complex agricultural questions and receive synthesized answers from 35+ research papers with confidence scores.

Regional Coverage

Specialized recommendations for 15+ Indian states including Punjab, Haryana, UP, MP, Rajasthan, and more.

Crop Advisory

Season-specific crop recommendations (Kharif, Rabi, Zaid) based on soil conditions and regional climate patterns.

Sustainable Farming

Recommendations for organic amendments, biofortification, and sustainable soil management practices.
